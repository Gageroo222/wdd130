Overview

Purpose

Splash White Water Rafting offers a thrilling, suspense filled trip through the great Tortuga River. Scenic views, abundant wildlife, and some crazy rapids are a guarantee. We can also Guarantee that you will get splashed! Choose from a small single-seat kayak, our 6 person raft, or for larger parties, our 18 seater will do the trick! Come and see what all the Splash is about!

Audience

Our target audience consists of adventure-seeking people and groups who love the outdoors and crave the thrill of white water rafting. They are typically between the ages of 14-45, and enjoy challenging themselves and connecting with nature. Most often, they are urban dwellers seeking to reconnect with nature and escape the daily work life through an adrenaline-pumping adventure. We cater to both beginners who are eager for something new, as well as more advanced rafters seeking some more challenging rapids. We strive to create lasting memories for individuals and families that will leave an exciting and memorable impression.
