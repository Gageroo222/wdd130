Wallows River

Our most popular trip by far, Wallows River, contains some of the absolute best scenery on the whole east coast. Our favorite spot along this stretch of river is Crazy Canyon, a snake-like pattern of river that passes through the narrow and steep walls of the Crazy Canyon. This is one of the few spots in the whole country that hosts the endangered "tri-toed mountain goat", an incredible site to see!

Free Fall Flat

Another exciting and wonderful stretch of the amazing wallows river is exclusively owned and upkept by us here at Splash Whitewater Rafting. Free Fall Flat is not for the faint of heart! With some of our absolute largest rapids and shtetches of shallows, you are guaranteed to get your heartbeat racing! This full day trip includes a lunch provided by your guide, on a small island on the river.

Shallows End

Shallows End offers what is easily our most beautiful stretch of the river, as well as being our most calm section of rapids. Dont let your guard down though! there are still many hidden drops and bumps along the way! Shallows End is the end of our stretch of the Wallows River, the trip ends just before the river opens up the the picturesque Grand Wallows Lake.
